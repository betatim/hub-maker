Open Source

Hub Hero is in the business of open source so you do not have to be. We have created and actively contribute to some of the most advanced projects in the PyData ecosystem. These projects include mybinder.org, JupyterHub and scikit-optimize.

We believe that open source products are the right choice for our clients. Open tools deliver a solid foundation, rapid innovation and are free of vendor lock-in.

By taking a leadership role in the open-source projects Hub Hero relies on we have the reputation and skills required to make the changes to these projects that our customers need.
