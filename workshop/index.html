Workshops

Hub Maker provides JupyterHubs for those who are teaching introductory programming, statistics or data-science in a workshop setting. All your students will have the same programming environment and datasets available to them without the hassle of setting up individual computers. Start teaching from the first minute.

The platform

Hub Maker provides JupyterHubs for teaching professionals. No matter if your students are professionals, high school or university students. It allows you to start with the content of your course on day one instead of having to get everyone setup first.

All your students will have the same environment, libraries and datasets available to them. The platform supports Python and R out of the box.

Based on the widely used, popular and open-source Jupyter technology all your students need is a web browser. While you could set it up yourself, we offer you peace of mind and our experience in operating these powerful tools reliably.

This offers is ideal for short workshops and summer schools. For details please email workshophub@wildtreetech.com.

The tools

You and your students will have access to Python and R notebooks. The cloud environment for each language comes pre-loaded with the most popular libraries in that language.

The Python environment comes with JupyterLab, Numpy, Scipy, matplotlib, scikit-learn and many more pre-installed.

The R environment comes with the Tidyverse pre-installed.

Features

Each student's online computing environment will be accessible through a unique login and password, have a home directory for storing work. Instructors can manage student's environments, preload data and course materials.
