Contact

To get in touch send us an email: hello@hubhero.net, we will reply within a business day. Let us be your Hub Hero.

Hub Hero provides JupyterHubs for teaching professionals. Our mission is to provide expert advice and service to empower everyone to benefit from the world class open-source tools that exist. Hub Hero is a service by Wild Tree Tech a Switzerland-based data engineering consultancy that embraces open source software and processes.

Engineering

Wild Tree Tech is based near Zurich, Switzerland. We deliver solid engineering for clients all around the world, with a focus on Jupyter technology.

Open Source

Wild Tree Tech is in the business of open source so you do not have to be. We have created and actively contribute to some of the most advanced projects in the PyData ecosystem. These projects include mybinder.org, JupyterHub and scikit-optimize.

By being part of large, international, and open communities we have a lot of experience managing diverse teams at scale and have insight to the trends of tomorrow.

By using open source products our clients are building on solid foundation, benefit from the development of new ideas, and are not locked in to a particular vendor.

The Team

Wild Tree Tech is owned and operated by Dr. Tim Head. Before creating Wild Tree Tech in 2016 he obtained a doctorate in experimental particle physics and worked at Fermilab, CERN and EPFL. In his free time he trains for triathlons.
